About Khandelwal Marketing

Khandelwal Marketing is the authorized distributor of India's leading cement brands - Ambuja Cement, ACC Cement, and Adani Cement - serving Balotra and surrounding districts in Rajasthan for over 20 years.

Founded by Mukesh Khandelwal and led by CEO Dinesh Mali, we have successfully completed major infrastructure projects across Rajasthan. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted partner for builders, contractors, and homeowners.
